# DeckForge Branding

## Purpose

This document defines the branding identity for DeckForge: the product
name, publisher attribution, and the chosen visual concept for the
application icon and mark.

---

# Identity

- **Product name:** DeckForge
- **Publisher attribution:** by RPG Frequencies

---

# Chosen Concept: Premium Hammer Strike

The icon concept is built around an **Ace of Spades** card motif,
reinterpreted through a "forged" visual language.

## Visual elements

- Ace of Spades card as the primary motif
- Thin brass outline around the card
- A forged (hammered/struck) treatment in the lower-right corner
- A partial Ace pip visible in the lower-right corner, consistent with
  the forged treatment

## Palette

- Charcoal
- Ivory
- Brass / copper
- Warm spark (accent)

## Tone

The concept should read as **modern craftsmanship** — precision tooling
and forged metal — rather than medieval fantasy blacksmithing. Avoid
tropes like anvils, flame swords, or fantasy heraldry.

---

# Production Requirements

- The icon **must remain legible at 32 px**. Fine detail in the forged
  corner and brass outline should be verified at small sizes, not just
  at full resolution.
- The current concept art is exploratory and **requires a simplified
  production pass** before it can be used as a final icon — reducing
  detail and reinforcing silhouette/contrast for small-size legibility.
- **Windows and macOS icon deliverables** will ultimately be needed
  (`.ico` and `.icns`, respectively, at their required size sets), once
  the production pass is complete.
